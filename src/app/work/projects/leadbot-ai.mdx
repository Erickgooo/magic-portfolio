## Overview

**LeadBot AI** is an AI-driven conversational system built to solve a real operational problem: a growing inbound pipeline that a small team couldn't handle manually.

Deployed via WhatsApp Business and integrated with Bitrix24 CRM, the system automated first-contact interactions for both B2B lead qualification and B2C customer service — freeing the sales team to focus exclusively on high-intent opportunities.

**Result: 85% of conversations handled autonomously. Zero additional headcount required.**

## The Problem

Artesa's commercial team was generating inbound leads through paid campaigns, but manual handling created three compounding issues:

- **Slow response times** that reduced conversion rates on warm leads
- **Data gaps** — salespeople started conversations without knowing the prospect's needs, volume, or location
- **Operational overload** — no customer service department existed, so the sales team absorbed every general inquiry

The business needed a system that could qualify B2B leads, handle B2C questions, and deliver only sales-ready opportunities to the team.

## The Solution

I designed and deployed LeadBot AI as a full automation layer between inbound traffic and the sales team.

### Infrastructure & Integrations

Bitrix24 doesn't support native WhatsApp connectivity, so I built the integration stack from the ground up:

- **BotSL IA** — Bitrix24 Marketplace app used to create the AI chatbot and connect open communication channels
- **Meta Business** — WhatsApp Business number linked directly to Artesa's Meta account for compliance and operational control

### AI Model Selection

The chatbot runs on **GPT-4o mini**, chosen for three specific reasons: high conversational accuracy, strong performance in customer service contexts, and cost efficiency at scale. This kept operational costs low without sacrificing quality.

### Knowledge Architecture

Instead of a rigid decision-tree flow, LeadBot AI uses natural language understanding — allowing it to handle unpredictable user inputs. To maintain accuracy and prevent hallucinations, I built a structured knowledge base using custom documentation:

- **Artesa Communication Guidelines** — tone of voice and brand rules
- **B2B Sales Manual** — qualification logic, information boundaries, conversation flow
- **B2C Sales Manual** — product FAQs, service scope, escalation criteria

Each document was developed and refined using **Gemini Pro** to ensure consistency across complex instruction sets.

### System Control

Using **OpenAI's Assistant Playground**, I configured detailed system instructions covering role definition, language rules, and data extraction logic for lead qualification. Temperature was set to `0.10` — prioritizing precision over creativity, which is critical in sales and customer service automation.

## Results

| Metric | Outcome |
|---|---|
| Conversations automated | 85% |
| Lead qualification speed | Instant (24/7) |
| Additional headcount needed | 0 |
| B2B leads delivered with context | Product interest, volume, location |
| Response time improvement | From hours to seconds |

LeadBot AI became a permanent operational system — not a pilot. It reduced friction in the commercial process, improved data quality for the sales team, and established customer service coverage the business didn't previously have.

## What This Demonstrates

This project reflects how I approach automation: start with the operational problem, design around real business rules, and measure success by what the team no longer has to do manually.

**AI doesn't replace people. It removes the work that prevents people from doing their best work.**
