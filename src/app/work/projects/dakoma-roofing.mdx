## Overview

Subcontracted by the CEO of Quick Metal Shop, I was brought in to modernize the digital presence of **Dakoma Roofing** — a traditional service business whose brand identity and marketing infrastructure had not kept pace with the quality of service they actually delivered.

I designed and executed a complete brand overhaul, integrated an automated SEO content generation pipeline, and created high-impact video advertising assets to reposition the company as a premium service provider in their local B2C market.

## The Challenge

The existing brand identity failed to communicate Dakoma Roofing's high-value proposition. There was no coherent visual language unifying their website, social channels, or ads. Additionally, their content operation was entirely manual, with no SEO strategy in place — leaving organic search visibility untapped in a highly competitive local market.

The business needed a total modernization of their digital channels and premium advertising assets to drive paid conversion.

## Strategic Solution

### Full Brand Overhaul
I executed a comprehensive rebranding initiative, delivering a structured Brand Manual that redefined:
- **Core color palette** — selected to communicate trust, durability, and premium positioning in the roofing/construction space.
- **Typography system** — establishing a consistent visual hierarchy across all touchpoints.
- **Logo refinement and usage guidelines** — ensuring the brand mark aligned with the company's actual market positioning.

The Brand Manual was immediately operationalized across website profiles, social media pages, and advertising campaigns, creating cohesive branding for the first time.

### AI-Powered SEO Automation
In May 2026, I integrated **Soro AI** into Dakoma Roofing's digital infrastructure — deploying the same automated blog generation system I had previously architected for Quick Metal Shop. This established a fully autonomous SEO content pipeline, enabling consistent, keyword-targeted blog publishing without ongoing manual effort.

<img src="/images/projects/Dakoma Roofing Project/dakomaproject2.jpg" alt="Dakoma Roofing Rebranding and Ads Showcase" />

### Premium AI-Generated Ad Creative
I developed a suite of high-production advertising assets using the **Higgsfield AI** ecosystem:
- **Kling 3.0** — for cinematic-quality video ad production, generating visually compelling footage that would typically require a full production crew and multi-day shoot.
- **Nano Banana Pro** — for AI avatar-driven video content, enabling spokesperson-style ads at a fraction of the traditional cost and production timeline.

The resulting ad creatives matched the elevated visual standard of the new brand identity — ensuring that paid media touchpoints reinforced the rebranding investment.
